Research Direction

My research focuses on Post-Quantum Cryptography and randomness in adversarial quantum environments.

Academic Insight

While developing my report on randomness in quantum cryptography, I sought conceptual clarification from established researchers.

Brief exchanges with Prof. Gilles Brassard and Prof. Nicolas Gisin strengthened the theoretical framing of the report. These were limited to conceptual discussion and not formal collaboration.

My interest in quantum cryptography developed alongside self-study in quantum mechanics, including material from Leonard Susskind’s Theoretical Minimum series.
